DAL MAKHNI

Ingredients of DAL MAKHNI

  • 2 Cups sabut urad dal
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ginger, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tsp shahi jeera
  • 1 tsp kasoori meethi
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cups cream
  • for garnishing green chillies (slit lengthwise)

STEPS TO MAKE DAL MAKHNI

Step 1:To the dal, add water, 1 Tbsp salt and ginger. Cook until dal becomes tender.
Step 2:In a heavy based pan, heat butter and oil. Add shahi jeera and kasoori methi.
Step 3: Turn off the heat, sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.            When they begin to splutter, add tomato puree, remaining salt, chilli powder
            and sugar.
Step 3:Stir-fry over high flame, till the oil separates.
Step 4:Add cooked dal and bring to boil. The consistency
            should be such that the dal should move around
            freely when stirred, otherwise add a little water.
Step 5:Leave to simmer, uncovered, till well blended.
            Stir-in cream and once it gets heated through,
            serve immediately, garnished with green chillies.
