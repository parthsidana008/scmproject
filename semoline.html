SEMOLINE

Ingredients

  • 600 ml milk (full fat or semi-skimmed)
  • 10-12 teaspoon semolina
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar

STEPS TO MAKE SEMOLINE

Step 1:Pour the milk into a pan and bring it to boil over a medium heat together with the sugar            and half of the cinnamon. Step 2:Add the semolina whisking continuously for about 2 minutes until the pudding thickens slightly            and bubbles. Step 3:Remove from the heat and transfer to a bowl. Step 4:Top it with the remaining cinnamon, or your choice of jam, fresh fruit or nuts.
