RICE AND FISH

Ingredients of Idli

  • Hake
  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Bay leaf
  • Tomato
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Salt

STEPS TO MAKE RICE FISH

Step 1: Place the fish in a saucepan with water seasoned with salt, a peeled small onion,
Step 2: In another saucepan, add 50 ml (1/4 cup) olive oil, chopped onion, peeled tomato cut
           into small chunks and the peeled garlic. Cook over low heat until the tomato start to break
           down. Then, add the fish pieces and the rice and season with a little salt and pepper. Stir,
           pour the cooking water and boil over medium-high heat. When starts boiling, reduce to low heat
           and cook about 10 to 12 minutes.
Step 3: Turn off the heat, sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.
