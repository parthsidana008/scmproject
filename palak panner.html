PALAK PANEER

Ingredients of Palak Paneer

  • 1 tsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 dried red chilli
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • few capsicum
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp cream
  • 250 grams paneer
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi

STEPS TO MAKE PALAK PANEER

Step 1:Firstly, in large vessel get the water to a rolling boil. once the water comes to a boil,
           add 1 bunch palak.
Step 2:Turn off the flame and dip the palak into boiling water completely.
Step 3:Rest the palak in hot water for 2 minutes or until the leaves are blanched well.
Step 4:Immediately, drop the palak leaves into ice-cold water. this helps to retain bright green colour.
Step 5:Now transfer the blanched palak into the mixer jar.
Step 6:Add 2 cloves garlic, 1 inch ginger, and 3 chilli.
Step 7:Blend to smooth puree without adding any water. keep aside.
Step 8:In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil, 1 tsp butter, 1 dried red chilli.
           1 tsp cumin and 1 bay leaf. saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
Step 9:Now add 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute until onions turn golden brown.
Step 10:Further keeping the flame on low, add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander
           powder and 1 tsp salt.
Step 11:Saute until the spices turn aromatic.
Step 12:Now add few capsicum and saute until the capsicum shrinks slightly.
Step 13:Further, add 1 tomato and cook until the tomato turns soft and mushy.
Step 14:Now add in prepared palak puree and cook well.
Step 15:Also, add 1 cup water and adjust the consistency as required.
Step 16:Now add 1 tbsp cream and 1 tbsp paneer. mix well making sure the consistency is
           attained as required.
Step 17:Add 250 grams paneer and simmer for 2 minutes.
Step 18:Also, add ½ tsp garam masala and 1 tsp kasuri methi. mix well.
Step 19:Finally, enjoy palak paneer recipe with roti or rice.
